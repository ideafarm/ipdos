Tutorial

This tutorial will eventually display a self guiding practice tree. Until I can put that together for you, just experiment with these actions until you see what is going on:

  1. Click the first sentence of a paragraph and then click "down1", which should take you back to where you were.
  2. Click a detail sentence (any sentence other than the first sentence) and then click "down2", which should take you back to where you were.
  3. Click up into the tree aways randomly. Note the title of the page. Click down1, noting where that same text now appears within the "containing" page.
  4. Climb around, paying attention to the title of each page and where it appears whenever you click "down1" or "down2" from it.
  5. Continue to play / explore until you see how an IdeaFarm (tm) Knowledge Tree works.
  6. Remember always that trees grow upwards. When climbing an IdeaFarm (tm) Knowledge Tree, you click on a sentence (branch) to climb UP onto it!
This is a fundamentally new kind of writing. Human beings have never been able to communicate in writing in three dimensions before; it simply cannot be done using paper. With a little bit of practice, I hope that you will see and enjoy the simplicity and power of this writing form.
